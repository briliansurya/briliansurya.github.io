About Me

Hi, I'm Brilian Surya Budi. I work at the intersection of data engineering and machine learning — building pipelines that turn messy, real-world data into reliable inputs for ML models. By day, I work as a data specialist at a national government agency, handling large-scale statistical data systems. By night, I do research on deep learning, graph neural networks, and adversarial robustness.

I'm fluent across the full ML stack — from designing ETL workflows and wrangling raw data, to training deep learning models with PyTorch, fine-tuning LLMs, and deploying them into production. I hold an M.Sc. from the University of Tsukuba with published research on adversarial attacks on temporal graph neural networks.

This mix means I don't just build models — I understand the data that feeds them. I'm available for freelance projects involving data pipelines, ML/DL development, and applied AI research.
